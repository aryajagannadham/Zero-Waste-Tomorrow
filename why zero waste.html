Why Zero Waste?

“Plastic is forever”. Single use plastic is banned. That is a great initiative. But is that enough? Plastic continues to suffocate the world in every possible way, scattered all around the land, in water, deep in soil and eventually inside out veins and cells. Every shop continues to display countless numbers of plastic packets with grains, pulses, spices, oils and snacks. Plastic bottles are discarded without a second thought as most of the cleaning products and personal hygiene products come in plastic bottles. With increasing population in cities like Bengaluru, there is tremendous pressure on the ecosystem due to of plastic waste from grocery. The waste management system of the city is very much strained due to huge quantities of plastic waste. The drains are congested and there is ever growing garbage piles in every nook and corner of the city. There is an urgent and unavoidable need to change the buying pattern and grocery shopping behaviour of the citizens of the city.
