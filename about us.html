About Us

Zero Waste Tomorrow is a project taken up by students of Kendriya Vidyalaya No 1 Jalahalli under National Children's Science Congress -2022 . The Focal Theme of NCSC 2022 is Understanding Ecosystem For Health And Well-being. Under the Sub Theme Technological Innovation For Ecosystem And Health, this project is taken up by Master Aman Kumar Singh and Master Rishabh R under the guidance of Mrs. Susmitha Ravi. The objectives of this website is to crate awareness about the hazards of plastic waste generated from grocery shopping and to give practical solutions for zero waste grocery shopping. Here you will find various shops which facilitate plastic free shopping. Also we have provided the contacts of milk supply and farms who either provide plastic free delivery or recycle their waste responsibly.
